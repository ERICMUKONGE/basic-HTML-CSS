EXECUTIVE SUMARRY

We are HBT consulting poultry farm ltd founded with:

VISION
To improve food production to meet the growing demand both locally and internationally

MISSION
To train farmers modern farming methods to improve production in quality eggs and chicken to meet the growing demand worldwide.

As HBT, we believe that people learn more in what they see and touch than what they hear, therefore, HBT consulting farm is going to be the center for training where farmers can come to learn by their eyes.All demonstrations will be conducted their, we are going to be rearing both layers for egg production, broilers for meat consumption and 1 day chicks.

HBT consulting team is passionate about this poultry farm as it is going to be their mother farm and source for training farmers to improve their food production

This poultry farm is to serve the following objectives:

  • Training farmers how to make a choice on the type of breed to rear depending on the resources available.
  • Training farmers the better ways and importance of in a poultry farm.
  • Training farmers the different mechanisms of taking care of the poultry to increase on production, with quality,less time and limited resources.

There is high demand for poultry such as eggs, chicks and chicken in hotels, schools,export etc but we have discovered that most farmers produce less products with low quality and use a lot of resources to produce them.

There fore, we decided to train farmers how to implement the modern farming methods which will help increase production, ensure quality and cost friendly in order to meet the high demand available.

We will need 100 million Ugand shillings, 40% on capital assets and 60% on operating costs.
We have been provided the land by the general manager of HBT consulting for a lease of 5years at a free cost and we are soliciting for the 100m from the HBT board members.
We are going to enter into business relationships with all the regions around us such as Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, Congo, Southern Sudan and many more to ensure steady market for our product.

Training will be conducted at our farm and visiting other farmer's farms to find out how they are progressing so that guidence can be provided where necessary.Our activities, brand,services and products are to be advertised on social media platforms such as televisions, radios stations ,facebook, instagram in order to make our services known to all people.
We expect to earn 1billion by the 3rd year and 4b by the 5th year of operation after tax.We are going to generate revenues from selling eggs, broiler chicken for meat and chicks to poultry farms.

COMPANY OVERVIEW

HISTORY

HBT consulting poultry farm ltd is a business formed to work as the center for practical poultry training .Farmers will have hands on training, this is aimed at improving food production in the country by training farmers how to make choices on the type of breed to rear, train farmers the importance of vaccination and teach them better mechanisms in farming to ensure that they produce high quality eggs, 1day chicks and chicken.
